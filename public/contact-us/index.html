Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

We understand that booking a mobile massage may come with questions, especially if it’s your first time. Below are some of the most common questions we receive from our clients. If you need more details, feel free to message us anytime — we’re happy to help.

Do you really operate 24/7?
Yes. Seven Angels Massage accepts bookings 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including late nights, early mornings, weekends, and holidays. Availability depends on therapist scheduling and your location within Metro Manila, but we are always reachable.
What areas do you serve?
We provide mobile massage services across Metro Manila, including condos, hotels, serviced apartments, and private residences. If you’re unsure whether your location is covered, just send us a message and we’ll gladly confirm.
What is the minimum massage duration?
All massage sessions require a minimum of 2 hours per therapist. This ensures a proper, relaxing experience and allows enough time for setup, treatment, and post-session care.
Do therapists bring towels, linens, or a massage table?
For hygiene and comfort reasons, clients provide clean towels or linens. We also do not bring a massage table. Sessions are set up using a suitable space within your premises, such as your bed, sofa, or a clean floor area with your own comfortable mat or covering. Our therapists bring essential massage supplies, including oils, and will help arrange the space properly for a relaxing session.
Are your therapists professional and background-checked?
Yes. All our therapists are skilled, experienced, and carefully screened. We prioritize professionalism, respectful service, and client comfort at all times.
Do you accept last-minute or same-day bookings?
Yes, we do accept last-minute and same-day bookings, subject to therapist availability in your area. For urgent requests, messaging us directly is the fastest way to check availability.
How do I confirm my booking?
Simply send us your booking details via your preferred messaging app. Once we confirm therapist availability, we’ll reply with the next steps to finalize your booking. Our team responds as quickly as possible, 24/7.

If you have a question that isn’t listed here, don’t hesitate to reach out — we’re always happy to assist.
