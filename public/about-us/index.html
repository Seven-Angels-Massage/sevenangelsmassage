Our Story: Calm, Delivered with Intention

Seven Angels Massage (SAM) is the city’s premier all-female mobile massage team, dedicated to bringing relaxation and comfort right to your doorstep. Experience the warmth and skill of our professional masseuses as they melt away your stress with expert hands you’ll absolutely love. With SAM, every touch is designed to deliver pure satisfaction—transforming every bit of tension into soothing pleasure.

SAM was founded in 2018 with a simple promise: to make high-quality, professional massage as easy as booking a late-night food delivery. What started as a small, all-female team in Makati has grown into a 24/7 mobile service trusted by locals, expats, and travelers across Metro Manila. Today, we focus on three things in every session: skilled hands, genuine Filipino warmth, and deep respect for your privacy and personal space. Whether you’re resting at home or staying in a hotel, our goal is to make your space feel like your own private spa.

Over the years, this commitment has been recognized beyond the Philippines. Seven Angels Massage was named Most Dedicated Personal Healthcare Services Provider – Philippines at the 2022 South East Asia Business Awards, and Best Hotel Mobile Massage Service 2023 – Luzon at the 2023 South East Asia Business Awards, both hosted by APAC Insider. Don’t wait any longer and indulge in the experience—reach out to us today! Feel free to ask us anything; we’re always happy to answer your questions and ensure you will have a first-class massage experience.

How Your Seven Angels Massage Session Works

We want every booking to feel simple, safe, and predictable — especially if it’s your first time trying a mobile massage. Here’s exactly what to expect from the moment you reach out to us until you take your last deep breath after your session.

Step 1 – Reach Out

Message us via SMS, Viber, WhatsApp, or Telegram and share your preferred time, location, and service.

Step 2 – Confirm Your Booking

We’ll check therapist availability and send you a Fresha link, or we'll handle the booking through Fresha on our end, so you can secure your appointment with complete and accurate session details.

Step 3 – Prepare Your Space

Before your therapist arrives, please prepare a clean, comfortable area for the massage on the bed, with space for movement, proper lighting, and access to a bathroom.

Step 4 – Relax & Rebalance

Your therapist arrives on time, sets up discreetly, and walks you through the session. After the massage, we share light after-care reminders and help you arrange your next schedule if you wish.

Our Therapists, Our Promise

Our therapists are the heart of Seven Angels Massage. All are female, background-checked, and chosen for skill, professionalism, and their warm, respectful approach to client care. For privacy and safety, we do not publish personal photos or real names — instead, we uphold strict internal standards that ensure every session feels safe, clean, and genuinely therapeutic.

We don’t just offer mobile massage — we bring a calm, safe, and reliable wellness experience right to your space. Here’s what defines how we work and care for our clients.

Rigorous Screening & Selection

We carefully screen every therapist through identity checks, interviews, skills tests, and professionalism evaluations before they join the team.

Ongoing Training & Skill Development

Our team receives continuous training in Swedish, Shiatsu, Combination, Deep Tissue, and Four-Hands techniques — ensuring consistent quality every visit.

Respect, Boundary, & Confidentiality

Every session follows strict draping, communication, and privacy standards. Conversations and client details remain and are taken fully confidential.

Health, Hygiene, & Cleanliness

Therapists arrive in neat attire with sanitized hands, premium oils, and proper hygiene practices. We keep every session safe and soothing.
